How to Use

Setting a Timer

  • Enter time in minutes or use quick select buttons
  • Click "Start" or press Enter
  • Maximum time is 600 minutes (10 hours)
  • Quick select buttons are customizable in settings

During Countdown

  • Pause/Resume the timer
  • Cancel to stop the timer
  • Video will pause when timer expires

Pause at End

  • Open video and click "Pause at End"
  • Automatically sets timer to video end
  • On Netflix, it will pause at credits
